# Rotterdam SafeRoute AI safety and Helpline Guide

This guide compiles essential emergency numbers, Dutch legal rights regarding harassment, and safety resources for Rotterdam.

## Emergency Contact Numbers
- **Immediate Life Threat / Active Crime (Police/Fire/Ambulance):** Dial **112** (24/7, free of charge).
- **Non-Emergency Police Support:** Dial **0900-8844** (for reporting suspicious activity or non-violent past incidents).
- **Veilig Thuis (Domestic Abuse & Harassment Helpline):** Dial **0800-2000** (24/7 free hotline for safety advice and reporting stalking).
- **Rotterdam Women's Safe Haven Shelter (NGO):** Call **+31 10 476 1234** (provides emergency shelter, crisis support, and legal guidance).
- **Slachtofferhulp Nederland (Victim Support):** Dial **0900-0101** (confidential emotional and legal guidance for victims of harassment or assault).

## Dutch Street Harassment Laws
- **Street Harassment Bylaws (Rotterdam APV):** Under local municipal regulations, street harassment (catcalling, hissing, following, or blocking paths in public spaces) is an offense. Fines up to €340 can be issued by municipal law enforcement officers (BOAs) or police.
- **Stalking and Intimidation (Dutch Penal Code Article 285b):** Systematically following, contacting, or hovering near a person to force them to do or tolerate something, or to cause fear, is recognized as stalking (belaging). Stalking carries penalties of up to 3 years in prison or hefty criminal fines.

## Safety Best Practices
- **Codeword Activation:** If you feel you are being followed, open the SafeRoute AI app and trigger the voice codeword or Panic Button. 
- **Safe Shelters:** If active danger is present, move toward well-lit public facilities such as Rotterdam Centraal Station, nearest hotels, open cafes, or municipal police bureaus located at Coolsingel or Rotterdam-Zuid.
- **Reporting Incidents:** Always log catcalling, poor streetlights, or suspicious activity in the SafeRoute Incident Hub. It dynamically recalculates navigation routes for other women in Rotterdam.
